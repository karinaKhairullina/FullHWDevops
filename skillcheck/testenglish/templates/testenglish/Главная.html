We all know that English is the most popular language in the world. It is spoken by almost every sixth inhabitant of the planet Earth. In many countries, English is considered the second language after the national language. English is the main language of world communication, the language of international trade, tourism, politics, the Internet and more. If you want to travel around the globe without any problems, then you need to be able to talk to people from any country. But, even if you are not going abroad, knowing English is very useful. You will be able to read an article, a book or a review of a film in the original language, learn the culture of another country or understand what is sung in your favorite song.

On our website "Skill-Check" you will be able to take tests of different levels, and you will also find links to articles with useful information about learning English, audio and video in English, you will also be able to download the books you are interested in in English. We hope that regardless of the purpose for which you started learning English, the information received on our website will make learning it convenient and easy.
